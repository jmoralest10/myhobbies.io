Jonathan Morales Hobbies

Hello, I am Jonathan Morales and I will tell you about my personal hobbies. Everything I enjoy doing is revolved around soccer or football as it’s known around the world. As most Mexican’s, I grew with the sport in my home, and I feel in love with it. I have always enjoy playing to this date. I started coaching soccer at the age of 15, so I could become my two younger brothers coach. I enjoy watching both of them play on weekends and cheer them on. When I am home with some free time I spend time playing FIFA the video game.

Playing the Game

Even though I grew up loving the sport, I didn't start playing on a team until age 10. I only played in a competitive environment for a couple years because it took away the fun for me. I always wanted to play just for fun.I still play pick up games every Sunday.

Coaching the Game

I began coaching at age 15. My brothers team coach had left so I decided to take the job. It was at the recreational level but I believe I helped kids have a great understanding of the game. I also helped develop my two brothers. One plays college soccer at the moment and the other one is on path to do so as well. Currently taking a break from coaching. I hope to return soon but this time at the youth club level!
